Jose Manalo Net Worth | Celebrity Net Worth

Jose Manalo net worth and salary: Jose Manalo is a Filipino actor and comedian who has a net worth of $2 million. Jose Manalo was born in Manila, Philippines in February 1966. He is best known for starring in the variety television series Eat Bulaga! on the GMA Network. From 2011 to 2012 Manalo starred in the TV series The Jose & Wally Show Starring Vic Sotto as Jose. He has starred in many films including Oh My Ghost!, Atang Family, Scaregivers, Love on Line, My Little Bossings, My Big Bossing's Adventure, and My Bebe Love. Manalo won a Metro Manila Film Festival for Best Supporting Actor in 2005. He has also won other awards including Lifetime Achievement, Certified Comedy Concert Artist, Most Popular Novelty Singers, and Best Actor.

According to paperwork filed in his divorce case, Jose was worth around P100 Million, which is the same as around $2 million USD. He was ordered to pay his ex-wife half the value of his estate, roughly $1 million USD.
