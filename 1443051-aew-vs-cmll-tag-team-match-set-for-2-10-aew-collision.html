AEW vs. CMLL Tag Team Match Set For 2/10 AEW Collision

This week’s episode of AEW Collision will feature a star-studded tag team match.

As announced during the February 7 episode of AEW Dynamite, Jon Moxley and Claudio Castagnoli will face Star Jr. and Esfinge on Saturday. Moxley, Castagnoli, and the rest of Blackpool Combat Club have been feuding with CMLL stars in recent weeks, and this rivalry will continue on Collision.

Moxley, Castagnoli, and Bryan Danielson beat Hechicero, Voldador Jr., and Mascara Dorada on Wednesday.

The updated card is as follows:

  • Jon Moxley & Claudio Castagnoli vs. Esfinge & Star Jr.
  • : Orange Cassidy (c) vs. Tomohiro Ishii
  • We’ll hear from Adam Copeland

WrestleZone will have coverage of the show as it airs on Saturday.
