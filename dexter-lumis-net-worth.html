Dexter Lumis Net Worth

Dexter Lumis has an estimated net worth of $4 million. This is from his career as a wrestler. He is currently signed to WWE, where he performs on the Raw brand under the ring name, Dexter Luis.

In February 2019, it was announced that Lumis had signed a contract with WWE and he would report to the WWE Performance Centre. He made his debut at an NXT house show on March 16, losing to Fabian Aichner.

Lumis made his appearance since leaving WWE on June 11, 2022, at the National Wrestling Alliance pay-per-view Alwayz Ready. He completed in a fatal four-way match for the vacant NWA Worlds Heavyweight Championship against Trevor Murdoch, Nick Aldis, and Thom Latimer; the bout was won by Murdoch.

Lumis returned to WWE on August 8, 2022, episode of Raw. His unannounced return was preceded by subtle references such as a car wreck during the backstage segment as well as cops running to the source of trouble.
