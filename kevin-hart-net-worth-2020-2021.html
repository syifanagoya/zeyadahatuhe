Early Life

Kevin Darnell Hart was born in 1979 on 6th July to Nancy Hart and Henry Robert Witherspoon in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He also has a brother named Robert. He was brought up by his single mother, as his father was in jail most of Kevin’s childhood. He saw comedy as his escape from his family issues.

Kevin completed his schooling from Community College of Philadelphia. Later he moved to Massachusetts, where he worked as a Salesman. During this time, he also started pursuing a career as a Stand-Up comedian and started performing at different clubs in Philadelphia.

Personal life

Kevin Hart has married twice; he married his first wife Torrei Hart in 2003, whom he divorced in 2011. The couple had two children together named Heaven Hart and Hendrix Hart.

He married his second wife, Eniko Parrish, in 2016, and his children from his previous wedding were there to cheer their father. He even appointed his son as his best man at his second wedding.

This year Hart and Eniko have announced that they are expecting their first child together.

Unlike most comedians, Kevin Hart likes to keep himself in good shape. The multi-talented artist has turned his demeanor into an actor’s. However, he still ensures to do his stand-up shows every now and then.

Career

Kevin Hart started off his career as a Stand-Up comedian, in which he used to mock subjects like marriage, parenting, racism, self-deprecation, everyday life, African-American Culture, current events, etc.

In 2002 he made his on-screen debut appearing on both the television and the big screen.

Kevin has done Television serials like ‘Undeclared,’ ‘The Big House,’ ‘Modern Family,’ ‘Jake in Progress,’ ‘Tough Crowd with Colin Quinn,’ ‘Lip Sync Battle,’ and ‘Top Gear.’

He has also appeared in movies like ‘Scary Movie 3’, ‘Superhero Movie,’ ‘Kevin Hart: I am a Grown Little Man,’ ‘Kevin Hart: Seriously Funny,’ ‘Ride Along,’ ‘The Wedding Ringer,’ ‘Central Intelligence,’ ‘About Last Night,’ ‘Top Five,’ ‘School Dance,’ ‘Think Like a Man Too,’ and ‘Ride Along.’ He is amongst the few comedy artists who have released their stand-up albums, however luckily for Kevin; these films helped him in growing his reputation as a comedian.

He is currently starring as a lead in the reality TV series ‘Real Husbands of Hollywood.’

Awards & Achievements

Kevin Hart has won 3 ‘NAAPC Awards’, 1 ‘Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Award,’ 1 ‘Acapulco Black Film Festival Award for the Artist of the Year’, 2 ‘Teen Choice Awards,’ 1 ‘MTV Movie Award,’ 1 ‘Billboard Touring Award,’ 3 ‘People’s Choice Awards,’ and 3 ‘Kid’s Choice Awards.’ In 2015, his name was included in Time magazine as one of the ‘100 Most influential people in the world’.
