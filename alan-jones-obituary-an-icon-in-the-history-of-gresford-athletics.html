Alan Jones Obituary, An Icon In The History Of Gresford Athletics

Alan Jones Obituary, Death Cause – This morning we were devastated to hear about the demise of Alan “Sammy” Jones, and our hearts go out to his family and friends. Sammy, a great character in the sport of football, once served as the manager of Gresford Athletic, a club to which he committed over 20 years of his life. During his time there, the team won many championships. He leaves behind an imprint that will never be erased, both in the annals of the club’s history and in the memories of all those who had the honor of calling him a friend.

Sammy Jones was more than simply the manager of Gresford Athletic; he was the organization’s driving force. During his career, he led the squad to a number of triumphs, exhibiting a level of devotion and enthusiasm that was unequaled by anybody else. His leadership invigorated everyone, from the players to the spectators. The unrelenting dedication that he showed to the club was demonstrated by the fact that it was successful during his leadership. The influence of Sammy was felt much beyond the playing field. For many others, he served as a guide, a friend, and a model they could look up to.

Everyone he came in contact with found him endearing due to the wisdom, humility, and kindness he exuded. As we say our last goodbyes to Sammy Jones, we will always remember him as a guy who devoted his whole life to the game of football and to Gresford Athletic. His legacy will live on in perpetuity in the records of the club’s history as well as in the hearts of all those who had the honor of getting to know him. Sammy, may you finally rest in peace. You will be sorely missed, but know that the Gresford Athletic family will always consider your spirit to be a member of their own.
