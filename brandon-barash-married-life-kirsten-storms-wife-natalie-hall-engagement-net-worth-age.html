Brandon Barash is popularly known for playing Johnny Zacchara on General Hospital. He premiered on the medical drama on September 18, 2007. He initially plays the son of mob boss Anthony Zacchara and brother to Claudia Zacchara. But it is later revealed that Claudia and Anthony are in fact his mother and grandfather respectively.

Now, since his last appearance on GH was on January 26, 2016, what has Barash been up to since? Is he still married to his then co-star Kirsten Storms? What’s his net worth? Is he dating anyone? Find out all the above and more below.

Brandon Barash General Hospital

Barash debut on General Hospital was a well-received addition to the cast. Daytime Confidential ranked him 9 in their ‘Top 10 Newcomers of 2007’ post. The post further commended him for his talent and how effortlessly he was with his co-stars.

Brandon continued to show his skillful performances in other films and TV shows, since his exit from GH. These include Timeless (2017), Cradle Swapping (2017), Scorpion (2017), and Lucifer (2018).

Barash and his friend Tyler Poelle, who’s also an actor, started an online platform called The Fathership. Here, they give opinions, discuss, and share bits of their lives as single dads. Their slogan summarises it better,

“Two single dads figuring it out… one mess at a time.”

Brandon Barash relationship with wife Kirsten Storms

Back in August 2013, Barash and Kirsten Storms revealed to People magazine that they had secretly wed in June of that year. Interestingly, Storms is his former General Hospital co-star. She plays Maxie Jones on GH and has been since 2005.

On January 2014, Storms delivered their daughter, Harper Rose. After three years of marriage, the couple told Soap Opera Digest they had filed for divorce in 2016. Storms had this to say,

“We are splitting due to the cliché irreconcilable differences, but we’re splitting amicably… this was not a hasty decision… We went to therapy for a long time…”

Whereas Barash added that they felt raising Harper in two separate homes was, “a healthier choice for all of us.” Currently, Kirsten Storms is dating a Rock and roll musician by the name Elias Paul Reidy. And Barash has also gotten back to the dating scene.

He recently shared a photo of him and a lady that goes by the name Morgan Mitchell. And the photo’s caption is lyrics from the song ‘It’s Always Been You’ by Ray LaMontagne. All the best to these new couples.

Brandon Barash and Natalie Hall engagement

Long before Barash was romantically involved with Storms and Morgan, he was dating Natalie Hall. Natalie is a Canadian actor best known for her roles in All My Children and Pretty Little Liars.

Caption:- Brandon Barash with Natalie Hall

They had been dating for a year plus when Barash confirmed their engagement via Twitter on December 2011. He was 32 at the time while Natalie was 21. However, they split before getting married.

Brandon Barash net worth, age, height

Barash was born Brandon Joseph Barash on October 4, 1979, to Jerry Barash and Susan Gale in St. Louis, Missouri. He is 38 years of age and reportedly has a height of 6’1.

Barash studied Acting at the University of Southern California. While there, he attended Harvey Lembeck Comedy Workshop and starred in several theatrical productions. Between 2002 and 2004 he played Jamie in Gilmore Girls.

He has since got more roles in other TV shows and films like Major Crimes and recently Lucifer. Brandon Barash has an estimated net worth of $1 million.
