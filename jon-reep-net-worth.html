His journey from humble beginnings in Hickory, North Carolina, to becoming a recognized face on television and comedy stages across the country is a tale of relentless pursuit and innate talent.

Who is Jon Reep?

Jon Reep is an American stand-up comedian and actor known for his contemporary country point of view. He has gained popularity in comedy clubs, on TV, and in film. His notoriety increased significantly after winning the fifth season of NBC’s Last Comic Standing.

What are some notable roles he has played on television and in films?

On TV, he has appeared in series such as Eastbound and Down, Black-ish, and Jane The Virgin. He’s also known as the Hemi Guy from Dodge’s ad campaigns.

In films, he has been featured in Into the Storm and played Raymus, the pot-smoking farmer in Harold & Kumar Escape from Guantanamo Bay.

Yes, he was the winner of the fifth season of Last Comic Standing.
