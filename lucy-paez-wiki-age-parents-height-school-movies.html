Lucy Paez Early Life & Parents

Lucy Paez was born on September 20, 2008, in San Pedro Garza García City, in Nuevo León, Mexico. Her parents raised her in the bustling city of Monterrey, where Lucy’s passion for acting began to blossom.

Her interest in performing began when she was just five years old, while she was in Houston visiting family; where her father’s cousin, an actor, invited Lucy to audition for a theater company, and she quickly fell in love with the experience of musical theater. From that moment on, Paez knew that acting was her calling.

After her first taste of acting, Lucy began to pursue her passion more seriously. She studied at the Tribble School in Houston and later trained with the TUTS theater company. Back in Monterrey, Lucy enrolled in the Triple Threat academy, where she trained with renowned teachers Raymundo Lobo and Lily Gómez. She also took classes at the Diffusion of the Tec de Monterrey with teacher Luis Franco.
