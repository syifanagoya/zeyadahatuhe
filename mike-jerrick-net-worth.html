Mike Jerrick Finance Overview

Early Life and Career Beginnings

Mike Jerrick was born in Wichita, Kansas. He started in journalism after earning his Bachelor of Science in Journalism from the University of Kansas. His first job was at WIBW-TV in Topeka, Kansas. He quickly made a name for himself.

Major Career Milestones

Jerrick’s career is full of milestones. After WIBW-TV, he moved to WNYW in New York. Then he went to KPIX-TV in San Francisco. His work on Sci-Fi Buzz for the Sci-Fi Channel and World Entertainment Report for HBO made him known.

He hosted What’s News on America’s Talking and America After Hours on CNBC.

Prominent Television Hosting Roles

One of Jerrick’s most prominent roles was hosting Good Day Philadelphia, where he became a familiar face for morning news viewers. His work on The Morning Show with Mike & Juliet alongside Juliet Huddy from 2007 to 2009 is also significant.

This show was a popular morning program that added to his popularity and financial success. Additionally, Jerrick’s contributions to the Fox News Channel, including his role on Fox & Friends, have been notable parts of his career.
