Amina Sheikh Shared the News of her Second Wedding

Famous Actress Amina Sheikh shared the news of her second wedding with a picture of wedding rings on her Instagram account. She added the caption with the picture “Bismiallah”. She lives in Los Angeles, California with her daughter after her divorce from Mohib Mirza. Many Celebrities congratulated Amina Sheikh second wedding.
