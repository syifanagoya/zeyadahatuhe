What is Uday Kotak's Net Worth?

Uday Kotak is an Indian banker who has a net worth of $15 billion. Born on 15 March 1959, Uday Kotak serves as Executive Vice-Chairman and Managing Director of Kodak Mahindra Bank Limited, one of India's most successful banks. A graduate of University of Bombay who has also an MA of Business Administration, Kotak has made a team of highly skilled professionals who have been in charge of diverse businesses in his Kotak Mahindra group, which holds many international subsidiaries that manage a wide range of investment funds.

In 2006, he finished a 14-year partnership with Goldman Sachs by gaining its 25% share for $72 million. Kotak also serves as a member of the Board of Governors of the Indian Council for Research on International Economics Relation, Head of The Mahindra United World College of India, and member of the Board of Indian School of Business.
