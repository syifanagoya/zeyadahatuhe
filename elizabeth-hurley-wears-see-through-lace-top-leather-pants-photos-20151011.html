Elizabeth Hurley Wears See-Through Lace Top, Leather Pants: Photos

A true queen, on TV and IRL! Elizabeth Hurley stepped out to celebrate the second season premiere of The Royals on Monday, Nov. 9, at the Hoerle-Guggenheim Gallery in NYC, wearing a completely sheer, lace top that showcased her incredible body.

The British beauty, who turned 50 this year, teamed the see-through piece by Tamara Mellon, which gave a peek at her bandeau bra, with leather pants, a black clutch, Jacob & Co. diamonds, and butterfly-topped Tamara Mellon sandals.

Of course, it wouldn’t be a junket without the whole cast! Hurley, who plays the E! series’ Queen Helena, was joined by Alexandra Park (Princess Eleanor), William Moseley (Prince Liam), Jake Maskall (Cyrus), and Tom Austen (Eleanor’s bodyguard, Jasper), who all confirmed that the characters’ wild antics are only going to get crazier.

“The queen, I like to think has a good side,” Hurley told Us at the press junket earlier that day, offering a sneak peek at the season to come (which premieres Sunday, Nov. 15). “We get to see a bit more of it in Season Two, but unfortunately we also get to see her bad side — big time. So we get to walk a [fine line].” The Bedazzled actress deliciously added, “It’s good to be bad.”

But Hurley, who has one son, 13-year-old Damian Charles, dished on more than the plotlines — she also talked about the queen’s gloriously glam fashion. (No Duchess Kate hemlines observed here!)

“[The fashion] is actually very important. When you’re playing the Queen of England, you have to be better dressed than everybody. Your jewels have to be bigger and better, and they are this season,” she revealed. “We also decided to use more British designers in this season. I wear a lot of Victoria Beckham‘s dresses, some English shoes, and some big, big, big rocks.”
