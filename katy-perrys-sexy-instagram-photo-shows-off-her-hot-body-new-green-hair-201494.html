Katy Perry's Sexy Instagram Photo Shows a Lot of Leg

So hypnotizing, Katy! Katy Perry took to Instagram on Tuesday, April 8, to post a striking photo of herself. 

In the pic, The "E.T." singer, 29, wears a figure-hugging sequined gown. The dress, which transitions in shade from a rich blue at the neckline to a shimmering gold below the waist, leaves little to the imagination. 

Perry struck a strong pose in the photo, showing off the up-to-there side slit which offers a peek at the curve of her hip. While Perry captioned the image "Just like a casual Tuesday lewk," there is nothing casual about her get-up.

Because the scandalous dress apparently isn't enough of a statement, the newly single pop star rocks her fresh "slim-green" cropped hair, a look she debuted earlier this week for the spring season.
