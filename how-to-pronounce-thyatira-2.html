What is thyatira called today?

Thyateira (also Thyatira) (Ancient Greek: Θυάτειρα) was the name of an ancient Greek city in Asia Minor, now the modern Turkish city of Akhisar (“white castle”). The name is probably Lydian.

What does the name thyatira mean?

In Biblical Names the meaning of the name Thyatira is: A perfume, sacrifice of labor.
