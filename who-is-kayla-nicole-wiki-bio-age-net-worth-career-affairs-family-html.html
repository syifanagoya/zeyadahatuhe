Kayla Nicole is a prominent American Entertainment Journalist, On-Air Host, Promotional Model, and Social Media Personality based in Los Angeles, California. Travis Kelce’s Partner:

  • Kayla Nicole has been in a loving relationship with Travis Michael Kelce for over three years.
  • They made their romance public in May 2017 after attending Jeremy Maclin’s wedding together.
  • Kayla supports Travis by attending his football games, and she even shared a photo of him when he was honored at his high school’s Hall of Fame.

Wiki/Bio:

  • Kayla completed her education at Pepperdine University, majoring in broadcast journalism.

Physical Statistics:
